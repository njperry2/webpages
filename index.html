My name is Nicholas Perry, and I am an undergraduate junior majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering. The allure of this major for me is deeply rooted in my profound passion for mathematics, physics, and computers. From a young age, I've always been captivated by the magic of computations, spending countless hours in front of a computer screen. This blend of interests naturally guided me towards the field I'm in today, seeking to harness the power of both electrical systems and computational technology.

These sensors are utilized both for human patients and animals. The goal is to enhance health monitoring through technological advancements.
